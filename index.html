Programming basis. The builder uses 1:2 work-to-rest as a beginner starting point and 1:1 for trained users, keeps ballistic defaults conservative, and labels 20:10 sessions as timing formats rather than promising a specific training effect. The requested source traditions—ACSM/NSCA interval guidance, Verkhoshansky-style work-rest progression, and hardstyle kettlebell practice associated with Pavel Tsatsouline—informed these adaptations; named sessions are not endorsements.
